Welcome to Mega Super

About Us

Mega Super is proudly Sri Lankan, owned and operated over the last 2 years, delivering the freshest quality products, along with great value, serving thousands of Sri Lankans every day. Mega Super operates 4 supermarkets operating with the core purpose of improving the quality of life for the nation, the supermarkets are conveniently located across the country providing our shoppers with a world class retail experience. At Mega Super, we pride ourselves on being your neighborhood supermarket, dedicated to providing you with the freshest produce, highest quality groceries, and exceptional customer service. With multiple branches conveniently located throughout the country, we strive to be your one-stop destination for all your grocery needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to offer our customers the best shopping experience possible. We aim to achieve this by consistently providing top-notch products, maintaining clean and organized stores, and employing friendly and knowledgeable staff who are always ready to assist you.

Our Products

From farm-fresh vegetables to premium dairy, beverages and cosmetics, Mega Super is stocked with a wide array of high-quality products to fulfill all your culinary desires. We also feature a diverse selection of organic, gluten-free, and international items to cater to every dietary preference and taste.

Our Branches

Kandy Branch

Situated in a residential neighborhood, the Suburban Branch of Mega Super is a spacious store that serves families and suburbanites. It features a comprehensive grocery section with a diverse range of fresh produce, pantry staples, and household items

Colombo Branch

Located in the heart of the city, the Downtown Branch of Mega Super caters to busy urban dwellers. It's a compact store with a focus on convenience, offering a wide selection of grab-and-go meals, snacks, and ready-to-eat items for professionals on the move

Kegalle Branch

Situated near a university or college campus, the College Campus Branch of Mega Super caters to students, faculty, and staff members. This branch offers a range of affordable, convenient, budget-friendly groceries

Kundasale Branch

Situated in a suburban community, our spacious branches provide a shopping experience that's both convenient and delightful. With ample parking, expansive aisles, and a diverse range of offerings
